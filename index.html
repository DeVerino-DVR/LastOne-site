// Frequently asked

Before you buy.

I'm starting with few players — is Starter worth it for me? +
Yes. Starter at 390€/month gives you 128 slots, real anti-DDoS protection and a pro base you couldn't get renting solo from a classic host. You launch small, and you scale up when your community grows — zero downtime.
What is NOT included exactly? +
Only three things: your CFX subscription (mandatory, to purchase directly at Cfx.re), the premium phone if you want a more advanced one than the classic already included, and premium mappings. Everything else — base, scripts, hosting, DDoS, support, updates — is in your plan.
Can I switch tier while running? +
Yes — upgrade in 24h, zero downtime, no new setup. Downgrade takes effect at the end of the month. If your community blows up, you go up. If you slow down, you come back down.
Why 5 days for installation? +
Because we don't ship a generic template. Every server is provisioned, configured, tested and customized to your needs. You receive a product that works on day 5, not a half-built construction site that kinda works on day 1.
What do your devs actually do with the custom-dev hours? +
On Pro, Elite and Ultimate tiers you get a monthly hours credit (2h, 5h, 10h). Those hours are for coding whatever you want: new jobs, economy tweaks, custom events, integrations. Unused hours don't roll over.
What's your difference vs ZAP or a classic host? +
A host rents you an empty box. LastOne ships you a custom base developed over 10 years, pre-installed scripts, support handled by the devs who coded the system, and regular updates. You pay more, you save months of work.
Monthly or annual commitment? +
No commitment. You pay monthly, cancel any time before the end of the current month. If you want to prepay annually, you get -10% on the full plan.
